ORDER REMOVING CONDITION OF LICENSURE

Kiarash L. Mirkia, M.D. (Dr. Mirkia), License No. 12548, appeared before the Nevada
State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly scheduled meeting on
September 15, 2023, requesting removal of the conditions currently affecting his license to
practice medicine in the State of Nevada, Board members Aury Nagy, M.D., President, Nicola
(Nick) M. Spiritos, M.D., F.A.C.O.G, Vice President, Ms. Maggie Arias-Petrel, Secretary-Treasurer,
Bret W. Frey, M.D., Ms. Pamela J. Beal, Col. Eric D. Wade, USAF, (Ret.), Carl N. Williams, Jr.,
M.D., FACS, and Inwin Simon, M1

FACS were present. After considering Dr. Mirkia’s request
for removal of the conditions of his continued licensure in the State of Nevada, the Board enters the
following Order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that any and all conditions currently on Dr. Mirkia’s
medical license are hereby removed. Dr. Mirkia’s license (No. 12548) is now active without
conditions.

Dated this 26th day of September, 2023.
