COUNT I
NRS 630.304(1) — Obtaining License by Fraud or Misrepresentation

= All of the allegations contained in the above paragraphs are hereby incorporated by
reference as though fully set forth herein.

6. NRS 630.304(1) provides that obtaining, maintaining or renewing or attempting to
obtain, maintain or renew a license to practice medicine (or respiratory care) by bribery, fraud or
misrepresentation or by any false, misleading, inaccurate or incomplete statement is grounds for
initiating disciplinary action against a licensee.

7. As demonstrated by, but not limited to, the above-outlined facts, Respondent
obtained her license to practice respiratory care by a false, misleading, inaccurate or an incomplete
statement when she answered “No” to question #13 on her 2020 application to the Board for
licensure as a respiratory therapist.

8. By reason of the foregoing, Respondent is subject to discipline by the Board as
provided in NRS 630.352.

COUNT I
NAC 630.540(1) - Willful and Intentional False Statement in Applying for License

2. All of the allegations in the above paragraphs are hereby incorporated by
reference as though fully set forth herein.

10. Pursuant to NAC 630.540(1), a practitioner of respiratory care is subject to
discipline or denial of licensure for willfully and intentionally making a false or fraudulent

statement in renewing a license.

13. All of the allegations in the above paragraphs are hereby incorporated by reference
