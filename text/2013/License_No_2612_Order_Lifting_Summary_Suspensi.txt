ORDER LIFTING SUMMARY SUSPENSION AND
IMPOSING CONDITIONS OF LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE

The Investigative Committee (IC) of the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners
(Board), composed of Theodore B. Berndt,.M.D., Chairman, Valerie J. Clark, BSN, RHU,
LUTCF, Member, and Michael J. Fischer, M.D., Member, after reviewing materials presented to it
by Richard Alan Singer, M.D. (Dr. Singer) hereby lifts the summary suspension currently in place

and imposes the following terms and conditions of practice.

1. The summary suspension of Dr. Singer’s license to practice medicine is hereby
lifted.

2. Dr. Singer is permanently restricted from practicing anesthesiology.

3. Dr. Singer’s practice of medicine is confined to the Neuropathy and Pair Centers of

Las Vegas, 8084 W. Sahara, Ste. E, Las Vegas, Nevada 89117.
_ 4; Dr. Singer’s practice of medicine shall be monitored by Robert H. Odell, Jr., M.D.,
PhD. (Dr. Odell), the Chief Medical Officer of the Neuropathy and Pain Centers of Las Vegas.
5. Further, Dr. Singer’s practice of medicine is limited to performing history and
physical examinations and serving as patient coordinator. Dr. Singer shall not perform any
procedures, give injections or write prescriptions.

6. Dr. Singer shall not supervise any physician assistants, advance practice registered
nurses or medical assistants. |

7. Dr. Odell shall monitor Dr. Singer’s practice of medicine and shall report to the
Board through its compliance officer every two (2) weeks, in writing, regarding Dr. Singer’s
clinical performance. Should Dr. Odell become unable to report to the Board during the term of
this Order, whether by choice or unfortunate circumstance, Dr. Singer shall immediately cease and
desist the practice of medicine. Dr. Singer shall then be required to procure a new mentor to be
pre-approved by the IC who will report to the Board every two (2) weeks as detailed above. At
such time as the IC approves the new mentor, Dr. Singer may return to the limited practice of
medicine as detailed herein.

8. ‘Dr. Singer shall undergo another evaluation in six (6) months (as previously
undergone with the same evaluator and at Dr. Singer’s expense) with the evaluation results
provided to the IC within thirty (30) days of Dr. Singer’s receipt of said evaluation.

9. After the IC’s receipt of Dr. Singer’s six (6) month evaluation results, Dr. Singer
shall appear before the IC at its next regularly scheduled meeting to revisit the conditions of
practice imposed on Dr. Singer’s license to practice medicine in the state of Nevada.

10. Should Respondent fail to comply with any term or condition recited herein, the IC
shall be authorized to immediately suspend Respondent’s license to practice medicine in the state
of Nevada pending an order to show cause hearing, which will be duly noticed and scheduled.
Further, the IC may then have grounds, after notice and a hearing, to take disciplinary action
against Respondent, including but not limited to, potential revocation of Respondent’s license to
practice medicine in the state of Nevada and/or any other discipline authorized by

Nevada Revised Statutes Chapter 630 and Nevada Administrative Code Chapter 630.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
