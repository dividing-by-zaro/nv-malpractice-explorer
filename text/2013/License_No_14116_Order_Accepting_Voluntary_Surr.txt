ORDER ACCEPTING VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF LICENSE

The voluntary surrender of John Andrews Tafel, M.D.’s license to practice medicine in the

state of Nevada, came on before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) for
consideration at a regularly scheduled meeting of the Board on December 6, 2013 at the Board's
offices located at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, 89502, and by videoconference at
the Board’s offices located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard, Building A, Suite 1, Las Vegas,
Nevada 89118. Licensee John Andrews Tafel, M.D. was not present.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were: Beverly Neyland, M.D.,
Mrs. Sue Lowden, Bashir Chowdhry, M.D., Wayne Hardwick, M.D. and
Rachakonda D. Prabhu, M.D. Colleen L. Platt, J.D., Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal
counsel to the Board.

The Board, having received Dr. Tafel's wallet license and affidavit and considering those
materials provided, enters the following order: |
