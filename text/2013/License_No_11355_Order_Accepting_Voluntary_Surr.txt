ORDER ACCEPTING VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF LICENSE

The voluntary surrender of the license to practice medicine of
Vinay Kumar Bararia, M.D., came on before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners,
hereinafter “Board,” for consideration at a regularly scheduled meeting of the Board on
March 8, 2013, at the Board’s offices located at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada,
89502, and by video conference at the offices of the Nevada State Board of
Dental Examiners located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard, Building A, Suite 1,
Las Vegas, Nevada 89118. Licensee Vinay Kumar Bararia, M.D. (Dr. Bararia) was not present.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were:
Benjamin J. Rodriguez, M.D., Beverly A. Neyland, M.D., Ms. Donna A. Ruthe,
Mrs. Sue Lowden, Bashir Chowdhry, M.D. and Wayne Hardwick, M.D. Harry B. Ward, J.D.,
Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal counsel to the Board.

The Board, having received Dr. Bararia’s wallet license, wall certificate and affidavit and
after considering those materials provided, enters the following order:
Ml
HI



IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the voluntary surrender of Dr. Bararia’s license to
practice medicine in the state of Nevada is accepted. This voluntary surrender is considered to
have been made while under investigation.
