ORDER EXTENDING SUMMARY SUSPENSION PENDING
RESOLUTION OF FORMAL AND/OR FIRST AMENDED COMPLAINT

The Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board), by and through its Investigative

Committee (IC), composed of Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., Chairman, Valerie J. Clark,
BSN, RHU, LUTCF, Member, and Michael J. Fischer, M.D., Member, hereby orders the
extension of the suspension of Emmanuel Galang Acosta, M.D.’s (Dr. Acosta) license to practice
medicine in the state of Nevada. This Order is made and based upon Nevada Revised Statutes
(NRS) 630.326, a review of the pleadings in this matter, and the hearing officer’s
Recommendation to the Board for Order Extending Summary Suspension Pending Further
Disciplinary Proceedings, or Order of the Investigative Committee or Board (Recommendation).
Previously, Dr. Acosta’s license was suspended by IC order on August 13, 2012. A Board
hearing addressing the propriety of continuing the suspension was held before a hearing officer on
April 24, 2013. The hearing officer’s Recommendation was submitted to the IC thereafter. A
formal Complaint alleging a violation of the Nevada Medical Practice Act, NRS Chapter 630 and
Nevada Administrative Code (NAC) Chapter 630, was previously filed on August 13, 2012,
Case No. 12-26709-1, and was served pursuant to NRS 630.344. A First Amended Complaint
