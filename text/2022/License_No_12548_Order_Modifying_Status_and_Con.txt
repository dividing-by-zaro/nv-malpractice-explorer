ORDER MODIFYING STATUS AND CONDITIONS OF LICENSE

On December 4, 2020, a Settlement Agreement (Agreement) was entered into by the
Licensee and the Investigative Committee (IC) wherein it was agreed that Kiarash L. Mirkia,
M.D. (Dr. Mirkia) would be subject to a term of probation for “not less than twenty-four (24)
months” from the date of acceptance and approval of the Agreement. See Settlement Agreement,
p. 4 at 5(c). This probationary period was scheduled to expire on December 4, 2022.
Subsequently, Dr. Mirkia requested that his license be deemed “Inactive” and his licensure status
was updated to “Inactive — Probation.”

On September 16, 2022, Dr. Mirkia personally appeared before the Nevada State Board of
Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly scheduled meeting and requested a change to the status
of his medical license. He requested that his license status be changed from “Inactive — Probation”
to “Active.”

After considering the request and discussion, the Board enters the following order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Dr. Mirkia’s probation is terminated. His license status
shall be changed to “Active — With Conditions.” The condition placed on Dr. Mirkia’s license is
