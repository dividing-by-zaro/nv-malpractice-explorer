ORDER ACCEPTING VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF LICENSE

The voluntary surrender of Horace Paul Guerra, IV, M.D.’s license to practice medicine in

the State of Nevada came on regularly before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners
(Board) for consideration at a regularly scheduled meeting of the Board on November 30, 2018,
located at the Capital Room, Renaissance Las Vegas Hotel, 3400 Paradise Road, Las Vegas, NV
89169, and by videoconference at the Board’s offices located at 9600 Gateway Drive, Reno, NV
89521. Licensee Horace Paul Guerra, IV, M.D. was not present.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were: Rachakonda D. Prabhu,
M.D., Wayne Hardwick, M.D., Mr. Neil M. Duxbury, Ms. Sandy Peltyn, Victor M. Muro, M.D.,
Ms. April Mastroluca, Aury Nagy, M.D., Michael C. Edwards, M.D., FACS, and Weldon Havins,
M.D, J.D. Henna Rasul, J.D., Senior Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal counsel to the
Board. The Board, having received Dr. Guerra’s written, sworn affidavit, voted to accept such,
and now enters the following order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the voluntary surrender of the medical license of
Horace Paul Guerra, IV, M.D., is hereby accepted pursuant to NAC 630.240, effective
immediately.

DATED this 30" day of November, 2018.
