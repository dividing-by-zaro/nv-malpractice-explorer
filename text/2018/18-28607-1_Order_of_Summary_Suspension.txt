ORDER OF SUMMARY SUSPENSION

Prior to the preparation of this Order of Summary Suspension, Nevada State Board of
Medical Examiners (Board) staff presented evidence and information to an Investigative
Committee (IC) of the Board, composed of Rachakonda Prabhu, M.D., Ms. Sandy Peltyn and
Victor Muro, M.D., regarding Isaac John Hearne, M.D. (Dr. Hearne). Upon review of the
evidence and information presented by Board staff, the IC hereby IMMEDIATELY SUSPENDS
Dr. Hearne’s license to practice medicine in the state of Nevada pursuant to Nevada Revised
Statute (NRS) 630.326(1), based upon the following preliminary findings:

1, Dr. Hearne is a physician licensed to practice medicine in the state of Nevada
(License No. 10767) and was originally licensed on January 7, 2004. Dr. Hearne lists his specialty
with the Board as Ophthalmology.

2. On July 16, 2018, pursuant to NRS 630.311(1) the IC issued an Order For
Appearance (Order) whereby Dr. Hearne was required (“shall”) appear before the IC to discuss his
five (5) currently open investigations with the aforementioned IC members.

3. On August 8, 2018, Dr. Hearne did not appear as ordered.

4, On August 8, 2018, this IC has reasonably determined that the health, safety, or

welfare of the public and any patient served by Dr. Hearne is at risk of imminent or continued

mutually agree in writing to a different date and/or time; and,

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that pursuant to NRS 630.326(3), this IC requires Dr.
