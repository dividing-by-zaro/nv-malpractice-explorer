COUNT I
NRS 630.301(3) - Disciplinary Action by Another Licensing Board

6. All of the allegations contained in the above paragraphs are hereby incorporated by
reference as though fully set forth herein.

7. NRS_ 630.301(3) provides that any disciplinary action, including, without
limitation, the revocation, suspension, modification or limitation of a license to practice any type
of medicine, taken by another state is grounds for initiating disciplinary action.

8. The Iowa Board took disciplinary action against Respondent on August 28, 2020,
as evidenced by the lowa Action.

9. By reason of the foregoing, Respondent is subject to discipline by the Nevada
Board as provided in NRS 630.352.
