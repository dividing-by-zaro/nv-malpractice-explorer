ORDER MODIFYING CONDITIONS OF SETTLEMENT AGREEMEN’

James Bennett Gabroy, M.D. (Respondent), License No. 7601, personally appeared in Las
Vegas, Nevada, before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly
scheduled meeting on March 1, 2024, requesting modification of the terms of the Settlement
Agreement and Order in this case, filed on March 3, 2023 (March 2023 Order).

After considering the request, the Board enters the following order modifying the terms of
the March 2023 Order:

L The remaining balance of the fines due in Case No. 15-10986-1 in the amount of|
two thousand nine hundred nineteen dollars ($2,919) shall be paid in twenty-four (24) equal
payments of one hundred twenty-one dollars and sixty-three cents ($121.63), with the first
payment due April 1, 2024, and the last payment of one hundred twenty-one dollars and fifty-one
cents ($121.51) due by March 1, 2026.

2. The remaining balance of the fees and costs due in Case No. 15-10986-1 in the
amount of five thousand five hundred eighty-four dollars ($5,584) shall be paid in twenty-four
(24) equal payments of two hundred thirty-two dollars and sixty-seven cents ($232.67), with the
first payment due April 1, 2024, and the last payment of two hundred thirty-two dollars and fifty-
nine cents ($232.59) due by March 1, 2026.

3. Upon receipt of Respondent’s April 1, 2024 payments totaling $354.30,

Respondent’s license shall be removed from suspended status and placed on active status.
