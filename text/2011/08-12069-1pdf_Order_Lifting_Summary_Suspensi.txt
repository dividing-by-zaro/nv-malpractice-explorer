On August 30, 2011, the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) conducted a

telephonic Board meeting to consider the recommendations included in the Order Withdrawing
Order Scheduling Briefing Re Threshold Issues and Directing Investigative Committee to
Forthwith Schedule Post-Deprivation Hearing Issues as an Action Item with the Nevada State
Board of Medical Examiners, which was filed on July 26, 2011, and prepared by
Mr. Patrick Dolan, Esq., the underlying Hearing Officer to the matter.

A quorum of the members of the Board who constitute the adjudicative panel in this
matter, consisting of Javaid Anwar, M.D., Beverly A. Neyland, M.D., Ms. Donna A. Ruthe and
Mrs. Sue Lowden, Board Members, were present telephonically. Bradley O. Van Ry, Deputy
General Counsel for the Board, representing the Investigative Committee of the Board, was
present in the Board’s Reno office, and Jacob L. Hafter, Esq., representing
Dr. Kevin R. Buckwalter, was present telephonically. Additionally, Harry B. Ward, Deputy
Attorney General, was present telephonically, and served as counsel for the Board.

The adjudicative panel, having reviewed relevant materials related to the July 26, 2011
Hearing Officer’s Order, hearing advocacy from the respective parties, and after deliberation,
moved and voted unanimously in favor of lifting the summary suspension of Dr. Kevin R.
Buckwalter’s ability to prescribe, administer, or dispense controlled substances. Moreover, the
Board moved and voted unanimously in favor of having the underlying formal hearing related to
the matter be heard in front of the currently assigned Hearing Officer.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
