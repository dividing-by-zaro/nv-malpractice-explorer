ORDER

The above-entitled matter came before the Nevada State Board of Medical
Examiners, hereinafter “Board,” on Friday, March 11, 2011, at the Board’s offices
located at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, 89502, for a hearing on the
Order to Show Cause filed on February 4, 2011, against Marilyn Atkins, R.R.T.,
hereinafter referred to as “Respondent.” The record reflects that Respondent was
properly served with notice of the hearing in this matter but did not appear in either Reno
or Las Vegas or have a representative present on her behalf.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were:
Javaid Anwar, M.D., Beverly Neyland, M.D., Michael Fischer, M.D., Ms. Donna Ruthe
and Mrs. Sue Lowden. All other remaining members of the Board, being members of the
Investigative Committee that issued the Complaint in this matter and/or entered into a
Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement with Respondent, were excused from
participating and took no part in the proceedings of the Board. Keith D. Marcher, Chief
Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal counsel to the Board.

The Board, having received and read the Motion and Order to Show Cause filed
in this matter on February 4, 2011, having reviewed its attached exhibits and having
heard argument from counsel for the Investigative Committee as to why the revocation

of Respondent’s license, which was stayed pursuant to a Settlement, Waiver and

should be lifted and the revocation imposed due to Respondent's failure to comply with

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stay of revocation of the license of
