ORDER AMENDING SETTLEMENT AGREEMENT

Ronald David Smith, M.D. (Dr. Smith), License No. 11778, personally appeared in Las
Vegas, Nevada, before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly
scheduled meeting on March 7, 2025, requesting that the Board lift the probation of his license
imposed pursuant to the December 1, 2023, Settlement Agreement entered by the Board. Board
members Nicola (Nick) M. Spirtos, M.D., F.A.C.O.G, President, Bret W. Frey, M.D., Vice
President, Ms. Maggie Arias-Petrel, Secretary-Treasurer, Chowdhury H. Ahsan, M.D., Ph.D.,
FACC, Ms. Pamela J. Beal, Col. Eric D. Wade, USAF, (Ret.), Carl N. Williams, Jr., M.D., FACS,
Joseph Olivarez, P.A.-C, and Jason B. Farnsworth, RRT, MBA were present.

After considering Dr. Smith’s request, and good cause appearing, the Board enters the

following Order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:
1. The probationary status on Dr. Smith’s license is hereby removed.
2. Dr. Smith shall continue his participation in a PRN monitoring program through
June of 2026.
