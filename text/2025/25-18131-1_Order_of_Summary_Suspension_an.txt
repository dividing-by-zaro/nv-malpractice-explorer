RDER OF MARY PENSION AND NOTICE OF HEARING

The Investigative Committee! (IC) of the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners
(Board) hereby IMMEDIATELY SUMMARILY SUSPENDS the license of Dana Ralph Marks,
M.D. (Licensee), License No. 9358 from the practice of medicine, pursuant to the Board’s
authority to regulate the practice of medicine in the State of Nevada pursuant to Nevada Revised
Statutes (NRS) Chapter 630.

The IC issues this Order of Summary Suspension (Order) based on its determination that
the health, safety or welfare of the public and patients served by Licensee are at risk of imminent
harm pursuant to NRS 630.326(1).

Prior to the preparation of this Order, Board Staff presented to the IC the following:

1. Licensee is a medical doctor licensed to practice medicine in the State of Nevada
(License No. 9358). The Board issued his license on May 1, 2000.

2. On June 2, 2023, Licensee entered into a Stipulation and Order to Continue
Participation in a Substance Abuse Recovery and Monitoring Program (Stipulation and Order)
with the IC.

3. Per the terms of the Stipulation and Order, Licensee was to remain in a substance

abuse recovery and monitoring program, and per the terms of his contract was to refrain from any

4, On or about September 29, 2025, Licensee provided a hair sample to a laboratory

ORDER

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the medical license of DANA RALPH MARKS, M.D.
(License No. 9358) is SUMMARILY SUSPENDED until further order of the IC or Board, and
Licensee is prohibited from engaging in any acts that constitute the practice of medicine pursuant
to NRS 630.020; and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that a hearing on this matter is set for the 2nd day of
December, 2025, at 9:00 a.m., at the Board’s office located at 9600 Gateway Drive, Reno,
Nevada 89521, to determine whether this suspension may continue, unless the parties mutually
agree in writing to a different date and/or time. See NRS 630.326(2).
