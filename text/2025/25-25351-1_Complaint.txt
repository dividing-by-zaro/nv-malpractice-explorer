Respondent.

by and through Deonne E. Contine, General Counsel and attorney for the IC, having a reasonable
basis to believe that Kristin Adele Hestdalen, M.D. (Respondent) violated the provisions of Nevada
Revised Statutes (NRS) Chapter 630 and Nevada Administrative Code (NAC) Chapter 630
(collectively, the Medical Practice Act), hereby issues its Complaint, stating the IC's charges and
allegations as follows:

1. Respondent was at all times relative to this Complaint a medical doctor holding an
active license to practice medicine in the State of Nevada (License No. 10215), Respondent was
originally licensed by the Board on July 1, 2002.

2. On June 7, 2024, Respondent appeared before the Board to request that her license
status be changed from inactive to active and to begin actively practicing again under a
preceptorship plan.

3. The Board entered an Order granting Respondent's request on June 17, 2024, on the
condition that she would participate in a twelve (12) month Preceptorship Plan with Byron
Czemiski, M.D., a Senior Psychiatrist at Lakes Crossing where she would work. Dr. Czerniski

COUNT I
NRS 630,3065(2)(a) - Failing to Comply with a Lawful Order of the Board

6. All of the allegations contained in the above paragraphs are hereby incorporated by
reference as though fully set forth herein.

7. NRS_630.3065(2)(a) provides that knowingly or willfully failing to comply with a
Board order is grounds for initiating disciplinary action.

8. Respondent failed to comply with the Board’s Order dated June 17, 2024.

9. By reason of the foregoing, Respondent is subject to discipline by the Board as
provided in NRS 630.352.
