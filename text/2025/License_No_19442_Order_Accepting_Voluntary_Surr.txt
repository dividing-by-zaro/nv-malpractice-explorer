At its regularly scheduled meeting on June 6, 2025, pursuant to NAC 630.240(3)(a), the
Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) considered the sworn written request of Steven
Wayne Powell, M.D., License No. 19442 (Dr. Powell) to voluntarily surrender his license to
practice medicine in the State of Nevada while under investigation (Voluntary Surrender). See
Exhibit 1. Board members Nicola (Nick) M. Spirtos, M.D., F.A.C.O.G, President, Bret W Frey,
M.D., Vice President, Ms. Maggie Arias-Petrel, Secretary-Treasurer, Chowdhury H. Ahsan, M.D.,
Ph.D., FACC, Ms. Pamela J. Beal, Col. Eric D. Wade, USAF, (Ret.), Carl N. Williams, Jr., M.D.,
FACS, Joseph Olivarez, P.A.-C, and Jason B. Famsworth, RRT, MBA, were present. After
considering Dr. Powell’s request, the Board enters the following Order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Voluntary Surrender of Dr. Powell is accepted and
that Dr. Powell is prohibited from applying for reinstatement of his license to practice medicine in
the State of Nevada until after June 6, 2035. See NAC 630.240(2).

DATED this 2nd day of July, 2025.
