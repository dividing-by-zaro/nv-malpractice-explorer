Ajumobi Charles Agu, M.D. (Dr. Agu), License No. 8857, personally appeared in Las
Vegas, Nevada, before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly

scheduled Board meeting on June 6, 2025, requesting that the Board modify part of the terms of the
Findings of Fact, Conclusions of Law, and Order, filed March 13, 2025, in this case (FFCL Order).
Board members Nicola (Nick) M. Spirtos, M.D., F.A.C.O.G, President, Bret W. Frey, M.D., Vice
President, Ms, Maggie Arias-Petrel, Secretary-Treasurer, Chowdhury H. Ahsan, M.D., Ph.D.,
FACC, Ms. Pamela J. Beal, Col. Eric D. Wade, USAF, (Ret.), Carl N. Williams, Jr. M.D., FACS,

Joseph Olivarez, P.A.-C, and Jason B. Farnsworth, RRT, MBA were present.

After considering Dr. Agu’s request, and good cause appearing, the Board enters the
following Order modifying the terms of the FFCL Order:

1. Dr. Agu currently owes the Board five thousand seven hundred fifty-three dollars
and sixty-seven cents ($5,753.67) in costs and two thousand five hundred dollars ($2,500) in fines
pursuant to the FFCL Order.

2. Dr. Agu’s request to modify the FFCL Order to allow additional time to make
payments is hereby GRANTED.

3. The FFCL Order is hereby modified to allow Dr. Agu to pay the costs and expenses
actually incurred in the investigation and prosecution of this case in the amount of five thousand
seven hundred fifty-three dollars and sixty-seven cents ($5,753.67) by way of six (6) payments. The
first of five (5) payments in the amount nine hundred fifty-eight dollars and ninety-five cents
($958.95) is due on or before June 30, 2025, the second payment is due on or before July 30, 2025,
the third payment is due on or before August 30, 2025, the fourth payment is due on or before
September 30, 2025, and the fifth payment is due on or before October 30, 2025. The sixth and
final payment of nine hundred fifty-eight dollars and ninety-two cents ($958.92) is due on or before
November 30, 2025.

4, The FFCL Order is hereby modified to allow Dr. Agu to pay the fine in the amount
of two thousand five hundred dollars ($2,500) by way of six (6) payments. The first of five (5)
payments in the amount of four hundred sixteen dollars and sixty-seven cents ($416.67) is due on
or before June 30, 2025, the second payment is due on or before July 30, 2025, the third payment is.
due on or before August 30, 2025, the fourth payment is due on or before September 30, 2025, and
the fifth payment is due on or before October 30, 2025. The sixth and final payment of four hundred
sixteen dollars and sixty-five cents ($416.65) is due on or before November 30, 2025.

5. The remainder of the terms contained in the FFCL Order shall remain in effect and
Dr. Agu must comply with these terms as stated,

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED.
