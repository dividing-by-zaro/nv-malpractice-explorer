ORDER TERMINATING REMAINING RESTRICTIONS & CONDITIONS
ON LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE -

Sean Phong-Quoe Su, M.D.; (Dr. Su), License No. 9013, personally appeared in Las
Vegas, Nevada before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly
scheduled meeting on December 4, 2015, requesting removal of the existing restrictions and
conditions regarding Dr. Su’s medical practice in the state of Nevada, After considering the
request and speaking with Dr. Su, the Board enters the following order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Board’s restrictions and conditions on Dr. Su’s
license to practice medicine in the state of Nevada are terminated and his license is restored to an
active, unrestricted status to practice medicine in the state of Nevada.

Dated this 4" day of December, 2015.
