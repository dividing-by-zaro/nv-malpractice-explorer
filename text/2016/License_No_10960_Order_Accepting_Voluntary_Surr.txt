The voluntary surrender of Ned Stoughton, M.D.’s license to practice medicine in the state

offices located at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, 89502, and by videoconference at
the Board’s offices located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard, Building A, Suite 1, Las Vegas.
Nevada 89118. Licensee Ned Stoughton, M.D. was not present.

Wayne Hardwick, M.D., Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., Rachakonda D, Prabhu, M.D., Sandy Peltyn,
M. Neil Duxbury, Victor M. Muro, M.D., and Ms. April Mastroluca. Henna Rasul, J.D., Senior
Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal counsel to the Board.
