ORDER ACCEPTING VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF LICENSE

The voluntary surrender of Emmanuel Acosta, M.D.’s license to practice medicine in the
state of Nevada, came on before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) for
consideration at a regularly scheduled meeting of the Board on June 5, 2015, at the Board's offices
located at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, 89502, and by videoconference at the
Board’s offices located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard, Building A, Suite 1, Las Vegas, Nevada
89118. Licensee Emmanuel Acosta, M.D. was not present.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were: Beverly A. Neyland, M.D.,
Bashir Chowdhry, M.D., Wayne Hardwick, M.D., Rachakonda D. Prabhu, M.D., Ann Wilkinson
and Sandy Peltyn. Henna Rasul, J.D., Senior Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal counsel to
the Board.

The Board, having received Dr. Acosta’s affidavit and considering those materials
provided, enters the following order:
