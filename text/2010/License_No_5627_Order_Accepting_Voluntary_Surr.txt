ORDER ACCEPTING VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF MEDICAL LICENSE

The consideration of the voluntary surrender of James Edward Beecham, M.D.'s license to

practice medicine came on before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners, hereinafter "Board",
on December 3, 2010 during a regularly scheduled meeting at the Board's offices located at
1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada 89502 and by video conference at the offices of the
Nevada State Board of Dental Examiners/Medical Examiners located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard,
Building A, Suite 1, Las Vegas, Nevada 89118. Dr. Beecham currently has a pending disciplinary
proceeding with the Board, case number 10-9883-1. Dr. Beecham was not present.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were: Benjamin Rodriguez, M.D.,
Javaid Anwar, M.D., Beverly Neyland, M.D., Michael Fischer, M.D., and Ms. Donna Ruthe. All other
Board members present were not members of the adjudicating panel hearing the matter. Deputy
Attorney General, Colleen Hemmingway, was also present and acted as legal counsel to the Board.

The Board, having received and reviewed Dr. Beecham's written and notarized surrender of
medical license number 5627, dated July 24, 2010, enters the following order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the voluntary surrender of James Edward Beecham, M.D.'s
license to practice medicine is hereby ACCEPTED.
