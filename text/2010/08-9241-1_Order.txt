ORDER

Respondent's Petition for Reconsideration and Modification of Settlement, Waiver
and Consent Agreement regarding the above-entitled matter came on before the
Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners, hereinafter “Board,” on December 3, 2010
during a regularly scheduled meeting at the Board’s offices located at 1105 Terminal
Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, 89502, and by video conference at the offices of the
Nevada State Board of Dental Examiners located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard,
Building A, Suite 1, Las Vegas, Nevada 89118. Respondent Eladio Carrera, M.D.,
hereinafter “Respondent,” was present at the meeting with his attorney, Andrea
Thorsteinsson, Esq.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were:
Ms. Valerie J. Clark, BSN, RHU, LUTCF; Beverly A. Neyland, M._D.,
Theodore Berndt, M.D., Michael Fischer, M.D., Ms. Donna Ruthe, and ad hoc Board
member, Ronald Kline, M.D., who was present in Las Vegas. All other Board members
present were not members of the adjudicating panel hearing the matter. Deputy Attorney
General, Colleen Hemmingway was also present and acted as legal counsel to the
Board.

The Board, having received and considered the Respondent's Petition for

DENIED.
