ORDER OF RELEASE FROM PROBATION

The above-entitled matter came on regularly for consideration before the Nevada State
Board of Medical Examiners, hereinafter “Board,” on March 5, 2010, at the Board’s offices, located
at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, on a Petition for Reconsideration and
Modification of Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement filed by Harold Tate, M.D., on
December 19, 2009. Dr. Tate was present with his counsel, Mark S. Ravis, Esq.

The members of the Board participating in consideration of the Petition were
Charles N. Held, M.D., Benjamin J. Rodriguez, M.D., Ms. Renee West, Javaid Anwar, M.D., Van
V. Heffner, Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., Michael J. Fischer, M.D. and Ms. Valerie Clark. All other
remaining members of the Board, who were not present, were excused from participating and took
no part in the proceedings of the Board.

The Board, after review and consideration of the Petition for Reconsideration and
Modification of Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement and after having an opportunity to hear
from Dr. Tate, the Board finds that Dr. Tate has complied with all the terms of the Settlement,
Waiver and Consent Agreement which was accepted and adopted by the Board on December 8,
2008 and that he may be released from the duration of his probationary term.

HI



IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Harold Tate, M.D. is deemed to have completed the terms

of the Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement and is hereby released from probation.

Dated this 5" day of March, 2010.
