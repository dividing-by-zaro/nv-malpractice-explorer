Valerie Clark, BNS, RHU, LUTCF, Member, regarding Anthony Cruz, C.R.T. Upon review of th¢
evidence and information presented by Board Staff, the Investigative Committee hereby
IMMEDIATELY SUSPENDS the license to practice respiratory care of Anthony Cruz, C.R.T
(hereinafter “Mr. Cruz”) pursuant to NRS 630.326(1) based upon the following preliminary findings:

1. Mr. Cruz is licensed to practice respiratory care in the state of Nevada and was
originally licensed on December 31, 2001.
2. On August 3, 2010 Mr. Cruz was working at Summerlin Hospital and was reported tq
be exhibiting signs of impairment. Mr. Cruz was requested to submit to a ten-panel urine drug screen
and he agreed to do so.
3. Results of the drug screen were forwarded to Summerlin Hospital a few days later and
showed that Mr. Cruz was positive for methamphetamine at the time of the test. |
4. On August 9, 2010, Mr. Cruz was terminated from Summerlin Hospital
The Investigative Committee believes that due to Mr. Cruz’s use of an illegal narcoti¢
substance, specifically methamphetamine, and being under its influence while providing respiratory
care, that the health, safety and welfare of the public is at imminent risk of harm and that a summary

suspension of Mr. Cruz’s license to practice respiratory care is necessary to remove said risk of
