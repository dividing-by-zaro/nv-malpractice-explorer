STIPULATION AND ORDER TO RESTRICT SCOPE OF PRACTICE
IT IS HEREBY STIPULATED by and between the parties hereto, the Investigative

Committee of the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board), composed of Charles N.
Held, M.D., Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., and Valerie J. Clark, BSN, RHU, LUTCF, by and through
Edward O. Cousineau, Deputy Executive Director for the Board and attorney for the Investigative
Committee, and Conrad R. Murray, M.D., by and through his counsel of record, that based upon a
criminal matter currently being prosecuted in the state of California related to alleged non-
therapeutic prescribing of medications that allegedly resulted in the death of a patient and based
upon the condition of bail in that criminal matter, Dr. Murray agrees that he is restricted from
prescribing or administering, except as mentioned below, any anesthetic agent normally
administered by an anesthesiologist, including but not limited to Propofol or any other heavy
sedative medication used as anesthesia, in the course of his medical practice in the State of
Nevada.

It is understood by the parties that Dr. Murray may still prescribe or administer any other
medications, including but not limited to pain and anti-anxiety medications or local or topical
anesthetics, in the course of his medical practice in the state of Nevada. And that Dr. Murray

may, in concert with other physicians, participate in medical treatment which involves the use of
