ORDER TERMINATING PROBATION

ON LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE

Douglas Stewart Lynch, PA-C (Mr. Lynch), License No. PA1486, personally appeared in
Las Vegas, Nevada, before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) at its regularly
scheduled meeting on December 1, 2017, requesting termination of the probationary status of his
license to practice medicine in the State of Nevada. After considering the request and speaking
with Mr. Lynch, and considering his ongoing compliance with the terms of his Settlement
Agreement dated September 11, 2015, and good cause appearing, the Board enters the following
order:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the probationary status on Mr. Lynch’s license to
practice medicine in the State of Nevada is terminated and his license is restored to an active
status, with the condition that Mr. Lynch shall remain bound by the terms of the Settlement
Agreement until they expire or until further order of the Board.

It is so ordered this day, December , 2017.
