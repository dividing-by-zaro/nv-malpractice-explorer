ORDER REINSTATING LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE WITH
RESTRICTION FROM PRACTICING GENERAL ANESTHESIA

RICHARD ALAN SINGER, M.D.,


License No. 2612

The Investigative Committee (IC) of the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners
(Board), composed of Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., Valerie J. Clark, BSN, RHU, LUTCF, and
Michael J. Fischer, M.D., after reviewing materials presented to it by Richard Alan Singer, M.D.
(Dr. Singer), and after meeting with Dr. Singer, hereby reinstates Dr. Singer’s license to practice
medicine in the state of Nevada with the following restriction.

1. Dr. Singer may not practice general anesthesia, including, but not limited to,
conscious sedation and/or deep sedation.

2. Dr. Singer will immediately notify the Board upon procuring employment as a
physician, and provide the address, telephone number and email address of such place of
employment pursuant to Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS) 630.254(2). Dr. Singer shall also
immediately notify the Board upon any change in his place of employment, and immediately
provide the new address, telephone number and email address of any new place of employment.

3. Should Dr. Singer fail to comply with any term or condition recited herein, the IC
shall be authorized to immediately suspend Dr. Singer’s license to practice medicine in the state of

Nevada pending an order to show cause hearing, which will be duly noticed and scheduled.
