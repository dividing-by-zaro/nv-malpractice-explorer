ORDER ACCEPTING VOLUNTARY SURRENDER OF LICENSE

The voluntary surrender of Carlos Tapalla Tisbe, M.D.’s license to practice medicine in the
state of Nevada, came on before the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners (Board) for
consideration at a regularly scheduled meeting of the Board on September 5, 2014, at the Board's
offices located at 1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, 89502, and by videoconference at
the Board’s offices located at 6010 S. Rainbow Boulevard, Building A, Suite 1, Las Vegas,
Nevada 89118. Licensee Carlos Tapalla Tisbe, M.D. was not present.

The members of the Board participating in the decision were: Michael J. Fischer, M.D.,
Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., Valerie J. Clark, BSN, RHU, LUTCF, Beverly A. Neyland, M.D.,
Bashir Chowdhry, M.D., Wayne Hardwick, M.D., Rachakonda D. Prabhu, M.D., Ann Wilkinson
and Sandy Peltyn. Colleen L. Platt, J.D., Deputy Attorney General, acted as legal counsel to the
Board.

The Board, having received Dr. Tisbe’s wallet license and affidavit and considering those
materials provided, enters the following order:

Hl
Hl


IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the voluntary surrender of the medical license of
Carlos Tapalla Tisbe, M.D. is hereby accepted pursuant to NAC 630.240, effective immediately.
This voluntary surrender is considered to have been made while under investigation.

DATED this 5 th day of September, 2014.
