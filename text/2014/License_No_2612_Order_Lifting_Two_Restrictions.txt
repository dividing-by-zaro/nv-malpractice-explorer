ORDER LIFTING TWO RESTRICTIONS OF
LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE AND MODIFYING TERMS OF
NEUROPSYCHOLOGY EVALUATION

The Investigative Committee (IC) of the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners
composed of Theodore B. Berndt, M.D., Chairman, Valerie J. Clark, BSN, RHU, LUTCF,
Member, and Michael J. Fischer, M.D., Member, at the time of authorizing the filing of this Order,
and after reviewing materials presented to it by Richard Alan Singer, M.D. (Dr. Singer), hereby
lifts two (2) restrictions of Dr. Singer’s license to practice medicine and modifies the terms of
Dr. Singer’s upcoming neuropsychology evaluation. The terms of this Order are effective the date
the Order is filed.

1. Dr. Singer may supervise physician assistants, advance practice registered nurses
and medical assistants. The former restriction against supervision of health care extenders is
hereby lifted.

2. Dr. Singer may perform peripheral nerve blocks, including, but not limited to, ankle
and nerve block injections. The former restriction against performing such injections is hereby
lifted.

3. Dr. Singer shall undergo a neuropsychology evaluation by a neuropsychologist on
or before July 5, 2014. The neuropsychologist shall be pre-approved by the IC. The evaluation
results shall be provided directly to the IC within 14 days of the evaluation and a copy shall be
provided to Dr. Singer within thirty (30) days of the IC’s receipt of said evaluation.

4. After the IC’s receipt of Dr. Singer’s s evaluation results, Dr. Singer shall appear
before the IC, at its next regularly scheduled meeting, to revisit the remaining conditions of
practice imposed on Dr. Singer’s license to practice medicine in the state of Nevada.

5, All other restrictions, terms and conditions on Dr. Singer’s license to practice
medicine contained in the Amended Order Lifting Summary Suspension and Imposing Conditions
of License to Practice Medicine filed January 6, 2014 remain in effect.

6. Should Dr. Singer fail to comply with any term or condition recited herein, the IC
shall be authorized to immediately suspend Dr. Singer’s license to practice medicine in the state of
Nevada pending an order to show cause hearing, which will be duly noticed and scheduled.
Further, the IC may then have grounds, after notice and a hearing, to take disciplinary action
against Dr. Singer, including, but not limited to, potential revocation of Dr. Singer’s license to
practice medicine in the state of Nevada and/or any other discipline authorized by Nevada Revised

Statutes Chapter 630 and Nevada Administrative Code Chapter 630.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
