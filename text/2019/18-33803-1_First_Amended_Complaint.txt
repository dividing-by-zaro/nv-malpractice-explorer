(NRS) Chapter 630 and Nevada Admunistrative Code (NAC) Chapter 630 (collectively the Medical
Practice Act). The IC alleges the following facts:

1, Respondent is licensed in Nevada in active status (License No.12786) and was
licensed by the Board on July 1, 2008.

one gross misdemeanor count.

Las Vegas Justice Court, this time alleging an elder enhancement on the felony count, and it also
included an additional gross misdemeanor count.
