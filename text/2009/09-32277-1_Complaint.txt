The Investigative Committee of the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners, composed
at the time of approval of the filing of the instant complaint, of Charles N. Held, M.D. and
Ms. Renee West and Theodore Berndt, M.D., having a reasonable basis to believe that
James Okoh, M.D., hereinafter referred to as Dr. Okoh, has violated the provisions of
NRS Chapter 630, hereby issues its formal Complaint, stating the Investigative Committee's
charges and allegations, as follows:

1. Dr. Okoh is currently licensed in active status, and was so licensed by the Nevada
State Board of Medical Examiners, on March 20, 2007 (License No. 12208), pursuant to the
provisions of Chapter 630 of the Nevada Revised Statutes, and at all times addressed herein was
so licensed.

2. Dr. Okoh was granted a license to practice medicine in the state of Florida on
December 6, 2000.

3. The Florida Department of Health filed an administrative complaint against
Dr. Okoh on February 17, 2009 alleging that Dr. Okoh had performed a wrong-site procedure in
July 2008, when he inserted a chest tube into a patientâ€™s right hemithorax when it was needed on
the left side.

4, Dr. Okoh entered into a settlement agreement containing multiple terms with the

Florida Department of Health on February 27, 2009 in which he admitted to the facts alleged in

5. Nevada Revised Status Section 630.301(3) provides that any disciplinary action
taken by another state is grounds for initiation of disciplinary action against a licensee.

6. The disciplinary action taken against Dr. Okoh by the Florida Department of Health
constitutes a violation of NRS 630.301(3) and accordingly Dr. Okoh is subject to discipline by the
Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners as provided in Section 630.352 of the Nevada Revised
Statutes.

WHEREFORE, the Investigative Committee prays:

1. That the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners fix a time and place for a
formal hearing;

2. That the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners give Dr. Okoh notice of the
charges herein against him, and when appropriate, the time and place set for the hearing, and the
possible sanctions against him;

3. That the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners determine what sanctions it to
impose for the violation or violations committed by Dr. Okoh; and

4. That the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners make, issue and serve on
Dr. Okoh its findings of facts, conclusions of law and order, in writing, that includes the sanctions
imposed; and
