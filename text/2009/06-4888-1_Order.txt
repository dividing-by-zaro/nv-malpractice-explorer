ORDER

A Request for Reinstatement and Final Resolution of Disciplinary Action was considered before
the adjudicating members of the Board on the 8" day of May 2009. Present were: Respondent
Frieda Fleischer, M.D. with her counsel, David Kimball, Esq. via video conference from Las Vegas,
NV, Board members Renee West, Sohail U. Anjum, M.D., Javaid Anwar, M.D., S. Daniel McBride,
M.D., and Van V. Heffner. Board member Beverly Neyland, M.D. was absent. Also in attendance were
Lyn Beggs, General Counsel and Christine M. Guerci-Nyhus, Chief Deputy Attorney General in Reno
and Albert Capanna, M.D. and Robert Comeau, M.D., both on behalf of Dr. Fleischer, via video
conference from Las Vegas, NV.

Following discussion and having heard from Dr. Fleischer, those present on her behalf and legal
staff of the Board, and with good cause appearing, the Nevada State Board of Medical Examiners
hereby enters its Order as follows:

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED, that Dr. Fleischer is hereby reinstated to practice as a physician
after completion of a successful peer review to be arranged by Board staff. Dr. Fleischer may not begin
practicing until notified by Board that she may do so; and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED, that if Dr. Fleischer successfully passes the peer review,
Dr. Fleischer may not practice obstetrics nor may she perform any surgical procedures. Dr. Fleischer
will be limited to practicing office based gynecology only and may not perform any procedures in the

office; and




20°


IT IS FURTHER ORDERED, that Dr. Fleischer shall be monitored by Dr. Robert Comeau.
Once Dr. Fleischer is allowed to practice medicine, Dr. Comeau shall review all of Dr. Fleischer’s
patient files within forty-eight hours of Dr. Fleischer seeing a patient for a period of four weeks
beginning on the date that Dr. Fleischer begins practicing. At the end of the first four weeks of
practice, Dr. Comeau shall provide the Board a written report regarding any concerns or opinion, and
the basis for such concerns or opinions, regarding Dr. Fleischer’s ability to practice gynecology.
Thereafter, Dr. Comeau shall provide such a report to the Board every two months based upon
continued monitoring of Dr. Fleischer; and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED, that the above stated conditions shall remain in effect until
further order of this Board; and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED, that the Complaint in this matter shall be dismissed without

prejudice.
