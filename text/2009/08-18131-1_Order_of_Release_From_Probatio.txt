ORDER OF RELEASE FROM PROBATION

The above-entitled matter came on regularly for consideration before the Nevada State Board
of Medical Examiners, hereinafter “Board,” on November 6, 2009, at the Board’s offices, located at
1105 Terminal Way, Suite 301, Reno, Nevada, on a Petition for Reconsideration and Modification
of Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement filed by Respondent, Dr. Dana Marks on
July 11, 2009, filed herein pursuant to Nevada’s Open Meeting Laws, NRS and NAC chapters 630,
and NRS Chapter 233B. Dr. Marks was presented with his counsel, Edward J. Lemons, Esq.

The members of the Board participating in consideration of the Petition were
Benjamin J. Rodriguez, M.D., Renee West, Javaid Anwar, M.D., Van V. Heffner,
Beverly A. Neyland, M.D., Theodore B. Berndt, M.D. and Michael J. Fischer, M.D. All other
remaining members of the Board, who were present, being members of the Investigative Committee
that originally issued the complaint in this matter, were excused from participating and took no part
in the proceedings of the Board.

The Board, after review and consideration of the Petition for Reconsideration and
Modification of Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement and after having an opportunity to hear
from Dr. Marks, the Board finds that Dr. Marks has complied with all the terms of the Settlement,
Waiver and Consent Agreement which was accepted and adopted by the Board on October 3, 2008

and that he may be released from the duration of his probationary term.



IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Dana Marks, M.D. is deemed to have completed the terms

of the Settlement, Waiver and Consent Agreement and is hereby released from probation.

Done in open session this 6" day of November, 2009.

UTA
